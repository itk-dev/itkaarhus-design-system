03 — Principles

001

Clarity over cleverness.

We build services for citizens — long-form Danish copy wins over decorative flourishes. Every component earns its place.

002

Open by default.

The design system is shared and reusable across products and partner kommuner. Decisions are documented, tokens are tokens.

003

The palette is the expression.

Seven faceted hues do the heavy lifting. No gradients, no decorative illustration, no emoji — the triangles and the colours are enough.
